# Gin Lemon Summer

## Ingredients:
- **2 oz** Gin
- **2/3** Lime
- **3/4 oz** Cointreau
- **1 Dash** Angostura
- **1 Dash** Bitter orange

## Instructions:
1. Add all ingredients to a cocktail shaker with ice
2. Shake well
3. Strain into a fresh cocktail glass
