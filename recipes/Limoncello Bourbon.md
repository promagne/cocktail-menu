# Limoncello Bourbon

## Ingredients:
- **1 oz** Bourbon
- **1 oz** Amaro
- **1 oz** Limoncello
- **3 Dashes** Angostura
- Orange Peel Oil
- Rosemary

## Instructions:
1. Fill a mixing glass with ice.
2. Add all of the ingredients.
3. Stir well until chilled.
4. Strain into a chilled glass.
5. Garnish with an orange peel.
