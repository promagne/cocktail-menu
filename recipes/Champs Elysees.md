# Champs-Élysees

## Ingredients:
- **2 oz** Cognac
- **½ oz** Green Chartreuse
- **¾ oz** Lemon Juice
- **½ oz** Cane Sugar Syrup
- **1 Dash** Angostura Bitters

## Instructions:
1. Shake all the ingredients with ice, then strain into a coupe. 
2. Garnish with the lemon twist.
