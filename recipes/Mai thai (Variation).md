# Mai Tai 

## Ingredients:
- **1.5 oz** Amber Rum  
- **1.5 oz** Coconut Rum (or Malibu)  
- **1 oz** Orange Juice  
- **1/4 oz** Orgeat Syrup  
- **1 oz** Cointreau  
- **0.5 oz** Lime Juice  

## Instructions:
1. Fill a shaker with ice.  
2. Add all the ingredients.  
3. Shake well until chilled.  
4. Strain into a glass filled with ice.
