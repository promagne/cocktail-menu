# CosmopoliBiks

## Ingredients:
- **1.5** Grey Goose
- **1 oz** Grapefruit Vodka
- **0.25 oz** Grenadine
- **0.75 oz** Cointreau
- **1.5 oz** Of Cranberry Juice
- **0.5 oz** Lime(or less)

## Instructions:
1. Shake with ice
2. In a coupe glass
