# Monica Drink Variante

## Ingredients:
- **1.5 oz** Rhum7 years
- **3/4 oz** St-Germain
- **1** dash Cherry Bitter
- Top of with Prosecco

## Instructions:
1. Shake Rum and St-Germain, cherry bitter with ice
2. Pour in a frozen coupe.
3. Top it off with Prosecco.
