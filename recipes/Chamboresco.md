# Chamboresco

## Ingredients:
- **2 oz** Bourbon
- **3/4 oz** Chambord
- **1/2 oz** Vanilla Syrup
- **Prosecco** to top

## Instructions:
1. Shake with ice
2. Pour in a coupe
3. Top with prosecco
