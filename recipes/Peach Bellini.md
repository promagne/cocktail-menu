# Peach Bellini

## Ingredients:
- **1 oz** Cointreau
- **3 oz** Jus De Peche
- Prosecco, To Top

## Instructions:
1. Shake all ingredients with ice.
2. Strain into a coupe glass.
3. Top with prosecco
