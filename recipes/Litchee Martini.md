# Litchee Martini

## Ingredients:
- **3 oz** Litchee Juice
- **2 oz** Gin(The Botanist)
- **3/4 oz** Dry Vermouth

## Instructions:
1. Add all ingredients to a mixing glass with ice
2. Stir well
3. Strain into a Martini glass.
4. Garnish with a litchee.
