# Rhumination

## Ingredients:
- **1 oz** Bourbon
- **1 oz** Rhum
- **3/4 oz** St-Germain
- **0.5 oz** Lemon
- **1.5 oz** Pineapple

## Instructions:
1. Shake with ice
2. Pour in a tiki glass
3. Garnish with a pineapple slice
