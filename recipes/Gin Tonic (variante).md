## Ingredients:
- **1.5 oz** Gin
- **1 oz** Vodka Grapefruit
- **0.5 oz** Tonic Cinnamon
- **200 ml** Tonic Water

## Instructions:
1. Add all ingredients to a mixing glass with ice
2. Stir well
3. Strain into a fresh cocktail glass
