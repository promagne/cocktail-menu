# Cosmopolitain

## Ingredients:
- **1 oz** Vodka Grey Goose
- **1/2 oz** Vodka Grapefruit
- **1/2 oz** Cointreau
- **1/2 oz** Lime Juice
- **0.75 oz** Cranberry Juice

## Instructions:
1. Shake with ice
2. Strain into a coupe glass
3. Garnish with a lemon twist
