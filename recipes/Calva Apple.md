# Calva Apple
  
## Ingredients:
- **1.5oz** Calva
- **0.5oz** Sweet vermouth
- **0.5oz** Symple syrup

## Instructions:
1. Torch in a flamming glass
2. Add little pieces of green apple
3. Serve and enjoy!
