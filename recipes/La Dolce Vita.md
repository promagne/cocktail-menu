# La Dolce Vita

## Ingredients:
- **2 oz** Chamomile-infused Old Overholt Rye(page 281)
- **¾ oz** Campari
- **½ oz** St-Germain

## Instructions:
Stir all the ingredients over ice, then strain into a double rocks glass over 1 large ice cube. No garnish.
