# La Dolce Vita

## Ingredients:
- **2 oz** Chamomile-infused Old Overholt Rye
- **¾ oz** Campari
- **½ oz** St-Germain

## Instructions:
1. Stir all the ingredients over ice
2. Strain into a double rocks glass over 1 large ice cube. 
3. No garnish.
