# French 75

## Ingredients:
- **1.5 oz** Gin
- **3/4 oz** St-Germain
- **1 oz** Blueberry Syrup
- **1/5 oz** Lemon
- Complete With Prosecco

## Instructions:
1. Shake with ice
2. Pour in a coupe
3. Top with Prosecco
4. Garnish with lemon twist.
