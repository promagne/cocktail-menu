# St Moritzino

## Ingredients:
- **1.5 oz** Vodka
- **1 oz** Cointreau/triple sec
- **1/3 oz** Sirop Orgeat
- **1 oz** Lemon Juice

## Instructions:
1. Shake with ice
2. Straing and pour in a coupe
4. Garnish with lemon twist.
