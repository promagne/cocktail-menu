# Madame Rouge

## Ingredients:
- **2 oz** Single Malt Whisky
- **1 oz** Amaro
- **2 oz** Brut Sparkling Wine

## Instructions
1. Place the whisky and Amaro in a mixing glass filled with ice, stir, and strain into a chilled champagne flute. 
2. Top with the sparkling wine.
