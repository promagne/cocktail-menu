# Perfect Solution Martini

## Ingredients:
- **2.5 oz** Gin
- **3/4 oz** Dry Vermouth
- Olive Oil

## Instructions:
1. Stir well 30seconds.
2. Strain into a coupe glass.
3. Garnish with an olive.
