# Tailspin

## Ingredients:
- **1.5 oz** Gin
- **1 oz** Chartreuse
- **1 oz** Red Vermouth Sweet
- **1 Dash** Orange bitter

## Instructions:
1. Rinse a coupe with campari
2. Add all the ingredients into a mixing glass with ice, and stir until well-chilled.
3. Strain into a chilled coupe glass.
