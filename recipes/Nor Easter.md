## Ingredients:

- **2 oz** Bourbon
- **0.5 oz** Lime Juice
- **0.5 oz** Maple Syrup
- Top of with Ginger Beer

## Instructions:

1. Add Bourbon, Lime Juice, Maple Syrup, into a mixing glass filled with ice.
2. Stir well until properly chilled (about 30 seconds).
3. Strain into a highball glass over a large ice cube.
4. Top off with Ginger Beer 
5. Serve and enjoy!
