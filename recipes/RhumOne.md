# Tropical Rum Cocktail

## Ingredients:
- **1 oz** Rum El Dorado
- **0.5 oz** Over Proof Rum
- **0.5 oz** Malibu
- **0.5 oz** Lime
- **0.5 oz** Coconut Cream
- **1 oz** Pineapple Juice
- **2 Dash** Angostura

## Instructions:
1. Shake 3 ice cube
2. In a glass with crushed ice
